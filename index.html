Xiaoman Lu

Xiaoman Lu is a PhD Candidate in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Warwick , under the supervision of Dr. Yu Guan. She is a member of Ubiquitous & Visual Computing Lab (UVLab).

Before joining UoW, she obtained her bachelor degree in Mathematics from the Northeastern University (China) in 2024.

Her work is at the intersection of computer graphics, machine learning, and information safety. In Xiaoman's research, she explores methods to implement deepfake detection, with a focus on developing effective, robust and generalised deepFakes detection algorithms.
